Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

"My spaghetti and meatballs recipe may be meat-free, but it's still delicious, thanks to hearty, smoky veggie balls and homemade marinara sauce."

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of spaghetti, cooked al dente
  • 2 medium zucchini, spiralized
  • Vegan meatballs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Mix the spaghetti and zucchini noodles and portion into 4 bowls.
  2. Top with 2 to 3 meatballs per bowl, generous scoops of marinara, basil, and drizzles of olive oil.
  3. Season to taste with more salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve.
